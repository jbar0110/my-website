Interesting Things I've Done In Class

Posted: October 15, 2017

I actually find several things in my classes interesting this semester. My Wines of the New World class taught me about the 25 different species of grapes, and we also taste different wines from all over the world. In my Restaurant Management class, we came up with our own restaurant ideas; we created our own menus and wrote a business plan which included a profit and loss statement. In Technology class, I'm learning a lot, and even got to create my own website!
